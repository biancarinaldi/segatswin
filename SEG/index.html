The Software Evolution Group at Swinburne (SEG @ Swinburne -- name pending) is a group based out of Swinburne University of Technology in Hawthorn, Victoria, Australia, conducting research in the area of Software Evolution [Add tooltip/link to definition]. More specifically, the group is investigating the behaviour of software developers and whether they actually practice and apply the principles and theory prescribed by software engineering literature.
