Bianca Rinaldi

Bianca is a 5th year student from Melbourne, Victoria, completing her Honours year in the Bachelor of Science (Professional Software Development) (Honours) course at Swinburne University of Technology.

She completed an Industry Based Learning year at the Defence Science and Technology Organisation in Canberra in 2008, which fostered her interest in research. During this year, she developed an Integrated Executable Architecture Framework with her supervisor and presented a paper at the SETE08 conference.

Her major research interests are in software architecture and evolution, in particular if software developers make use of model separated code, and what the implications of its use have on a project.

Outside of software development, Bianca plays football (soccer) and is an avid movie enthusiast.
